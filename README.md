# Banking Quiz Hub

A collection of self‑contained, interactive quiz apps for banking and finance courses. No server, no login, no data sent anywhere — everything runs in your browser.

## Courses Included

| Course | Questions | Topics |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| CPG Quiz Master | 500 | Credit Policy Guide |
| Basic Banking Operations | 189 | BBO — Teller, Clearing, TROPS, Fraud |
| Marketing of Financial Services | 120 | Sales, Marketing, Customer Retention |
| Accounting MCQ Quiz | 114 | Financial Accounting |
| Trade Finance Quiz | 105 | Trade Finance & International Trade |
| FGM Quiz | 98 | International Banking & Finance |
| AfCFTA & Intra‑African Trade | 70 | AfCFTA, PAPSS, Trade Barriers |
| ERM Quiz | 50 | Enterprise Risk Management |

## Features

- **Practice Mode** – instant feedback with explanations
- **Exam Mode** – timed assessment with countdown timer
- **Flashcard Revision** – flip cards for active recall
- **Missed‑Only** – review questions you got wrong
- **Full Review** – see all answers and explanations after each session
- **Persistent Progress** – missed questions saved in your browser

## How to Use

1. Visit the [Hub Page](https://dekache009.github.io/accesseltp_sbequiz)
2. Click any course card to start
3. Choose your mode, question count, and start!

## Local Use

Download any `.html` file and open it in your browser — it works offline.

## No Data Collected

All quizzes are static HTML/JavaScript. No user data is ever sent to any server.
